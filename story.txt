Once upon a time there was a little girl who lived in a castle. She had long silky red hair just like her mother.
Her name was Annabelle.The kingdom loved her. She would come outside the castle during the day and play with other
kids. One day, while playing in the castle courtyard, the trumpets started blaring. A mysterious traveler entered the castle gates. But it turned out the mysterious man was her long lost father who went to go buy milk...
